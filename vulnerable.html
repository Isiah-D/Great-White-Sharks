The Great White Needs your Help!

The Great White is the world's largest known predatory fish. This fact may suggest that the species should have no problem fending for itself, right? Unfortunately, the animal is decreasing in numbers and humans are responsible. The animal is hunted both for its fins and teeth, and often as a trophy in sport fishing. They also tend to get caught in nets used for commercial fishing, along with the meshes that protect beaches. The great white has a conservational status of "vulnerable".

You may be asking: "What can I do to help these awesome creatures?" That's easy. You can donate to a organization that fights to protect the ocean and the creatures that inhabit it. Projecet Aware is one of those organizations! There's also the Oceanic Preservation Society or OPS for short!
